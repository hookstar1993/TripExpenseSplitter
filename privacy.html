Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 17 September 2025

Dwarkvyn ("us", "we", or "our") operates the Expense Splitter web application and the corresponding Android mobile application (the "Service"). Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data.

1. Data Collection and Use

Our Service is a purely client-side application. This means all the code runs directly in your web browser or on your mobile device. We do not have a server or a database. **We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any of your personal data.**

2. Local Storage Usage

To provide a persistent experience, the Service uses your browser's `localStorage` or the equivalent local storage on your mobile device. This allows the data you enter—such as tour names, participant names, and expense details—to be saved directly on your own device. This data remains in your control, on your device, and is not sent to us or any third party.

You have full control over this data. You can clear it at any time by using the "Clear All Records" button within the application or by clearing the application's data through your device settings.

3. Third-Party Services

The Service does not use any third-party services that may collect information from you. We do not integrate with any analytics, advertising, or data-processing services.

4. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to anyone under the age of 13 ("Children"). We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under 13. Since our application does not collect any data at all, we do not collect data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with Personal Data (which is technically impossible via our Service), please contact us.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
