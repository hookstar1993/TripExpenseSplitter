Last Updated: 17 September 2025

Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information that is handled by our Expense Splitter tool and how it is used.

1. No Data Collection

Our Expense Splitter tool is a purely client-side application. This means all the code runs directly in your web browser. We do not have a server, a database, or any backend infrastructure. **We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.**

2. Local Storage Usage

To provide a persistent experience, the tool uses your browser's `localStorage` feature. This allows the data you enter—such as tour names, participant names, and expense details—to be saved directly on your own computer. This data remains in your browser and is not sent to us or any third party.

You have full control over this data. You can clear it at any time by using the "Clear All Records" button within the application or by clearing your browser's cache and site data for this page.

3. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of that data is your responsibility. We recommend using a secure computer and browser to protect your information.

4. Import/Export Functionality

The "Import Data" and "Export Data" features are designed to help you back up and restore your expense data. When you export data, a JSON file is created and saved to your local device. When you import, the tool reads a JSON file from your local device. At no point is this data transmitted over the internet to our servers.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us. However, please note that since we do not store your data, we cannot assist with data recovery or access requests.
